The start of the 2024 Season

The 2024 season started off on the 16th of February. Sligo Rovers played Bohemians away at Dalymount Park. Bohemians won the FA Cup in 2023, so many people thought it would be an easy win for them, also considering that Sligo Rovers came 8th in the table last year. Bohemians scored first, but Sligo Rovers went 2-1 up at halftime. The game ended in a draw with many people thinking the Sligo side deserved the win. Sligo's second game was played back home in The Showgrounds, against the runners-up last year, Derry City. The match ended in a 0-0 draw, leaving Sligo with just 2 points at the end of the second week.
